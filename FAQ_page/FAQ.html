FAQ

  • How long can I rent a costume?
    We offer 3-day and 8-day rentals. Longer rentals may be arranged on request.
  • What if I need to extend my rental?
    Contact us ASAP! Extensions may be available at a daily rate.
  • What happens if I return a costume late?
    Late returns cost $5 per extra day, up to the cost of an 8-day rental. After that, it counts as a buy ($60) and the costume is yours to keep!
  • Do I need to wash/clean the costume before returning it?
    “Nope, we’ll handle all the cleaning! Just return it in good condition.
  • What if something gets damaged?
    Minor wear is expected. Major damage or loss may result in a replacement fee.
  • How do fittings work?
    We meet you to take measurements and discuss your project, plus delivery, all on-campus!
  • Can you alter a costume for me?
    Yes! We offer small alterations (fit adjustments, hemming) as part of the rental process.
  • How long does a custom build take?
    Depends on complexity. Simple alterations can be done in a few days, while full builds may need 2-3 weeks.
  • Do you do cosplay commissions too?
    Yes! Whether it's for a con, a short film, or even a Halloween costume, we can build or guide your look. (Pricing depends on complexity.)
  • Can I use rentals for stunts (like fights, falls, or heavy action scenes)?
    Possibly! Since stunts can put extra stress on costumes, we'll discuss your project on a case-by-case basis, and if additional costs apply.
