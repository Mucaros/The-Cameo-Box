Every great story deserves the perfect outfit. The Cameo Box is a costume hub where filmmakers, theater makers, and storytellers can rent or buy film-ready attire without the stress of sourcing them alone. Founded at GMU on September 5, 2025, our mission is to make costuming simple, accessible, and affordable for creators and independent projects. Powered by a small team of four passionate designers and curators, we are committed to finding the best possible outfits, accessories, and wardrobes to bring your character to life, all while keeping things local and sustainable.
