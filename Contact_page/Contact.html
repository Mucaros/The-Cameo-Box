Contact us

Have a project in mind? We'd love to hear about it! The Cameo Box is here to take the stress out of costuming so you can focus on bringing your story to life. Not sure what to include? Here's what helps us help you:

  • Project type (film, theater, cosplay, etc.)
  • Timelines (start date, how long you'll need the fit)
  • Any specific looks, genres, or inspirations

You can also reach out with questions, ideas, or feedback! We'll follow up within 1-2 days with options, estimated pricing, and to schedule a meeting.
