Policies

  • Grace period: 1 Day (no fee). We get it, don’t stress! After grace: $5 / day
  • Max late fee: Up to the cost of an 8-day rental, ignoring grace period. After that, we treat it as a “buy”.
  • A 3-day rental ($15) returned on Day 6 costs $30. If you keep it until Day 10, it’s $45. An 8-day rental returned on Day 12 is $55, but after Day 12 it becomes a full buy at $60.
  • Damages? Accidents happen! Minor wear (loose thread, tiny stains) is fine, but major damage requires some fees to make sure we can keep giving out clothing!
  • Minor repairable damage: $10 to cover sewing/cleaning
  • Major damage/lost item: Replacement fee of $60

Cancellations?

  • Changed your mind? Just don’t need it anymore? Whatever the reason, you can cancel your order for a full refund, if you do so 48 hours before pickup!
  • Canceling 24-48 hours before gets you a 25% refund + 25% credit towards your next order!
  • Same-day cancellations see no refunds, as the order was reserved
