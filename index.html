Who?

Every great story deserves the perfect outfit. The Cameo Box is a costume hub where filmmakers, thespians, and storytellers can rent or buy film-ready attire without the stress of sourcing them alone. Founded at GMU on September 5, 2025, our mission is to make costuming simple, accessible, and affordable for creators and independent projects.

Powered by a small team of four passionate designers and curators, we are committed to finding the best possible outfits, accessories, and wardrobes to bring your character to life, all while keeping your ensemble local and sustainable.

  • Step 1:
  • Book a day for us to visit you and your team in person
  • Step 2:
  • We meet up with you, get measurements, and talk about your vision for your character(s)
  • Step 3:
  • We send you a concept design, and you confirm with us about anything you want refined
  • Step 4:
  • We schedule a day to deliver your costume!
